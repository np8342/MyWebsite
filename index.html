All about Basketball

What is basketball?

Basketball is a team sport. Two teams each try to score by shooting a ball through a hoop above the ground. The game is played on a rectangular floor called the court, and there is a hoop at each end.

How popular is it?

Basketball is a popular sport all around the world. It is popular in nearly every Asian, European, and American country. Basketball has even been designated as an Olympic sport. It is so popular that it has become the world's second-most popular team game, only after soccer.

When was it invented?

The game was invented by Springfield College instructor and graduate student James Naismith in 1891, and has grown into the worldwide athletic phenomenon we know it to be today.
